## What the Numbers Tell Us

**CityPlace dominates.** Seven of the top 20 buildings are in the CityPlace and Fort York area (21 Iceboat Ter, 151 Dan Leckie Way, 215/209/219/231 Fort York Blvd). This pocket of Toronto has more STR registrations than most Canadian cities.

**The ICE Condos (12 and 14 York St) are massive.** Combined, 12 York and 14 York have 281 registrations. Their location at York and Bremner, steps from Union Station and the waterfront, makes them a top choice for guests.

**Yorkville plays a different game.** 155 Yorkville Ave (Four Seasons Residences) has 94 registrations. Fewer than the downtown towers, but Yorkville units command significantly higher nightly rates. Luxury condos earn more per booking with less competition.

**251 Jarvis St is the wildcard.** With 196 registrations, it is the second most registered building in Toronto. Located at Jarvis and Dundas, this is not typically considered a premium area, but the high registration count suggests strong demand and a very STR-friendly condo board.
