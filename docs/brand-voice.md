# Brand Voice Guidelines

## Tone
- Professional but approachable
- Confident without being arrogant
- Helpful and educational
- Local and relatable (we're GTA-based, not a big corporation)

## Key Messages
1. **Maximize income** - "Earn 30-100% more"
2. **Stress-free** - "We handle everything while you collect rent"
3. **Transparent** - No hidden fees, simple pricing
4. **No lock-in** - 30-day cancellation, you own your listing
5. **Local expertise** - Locally owned, understand GTA market

## Words/Phrases We Use
- "Maximize your rental income"
- "Passive income"
- "Premium management"
- "Stress-free"
- "Transparent pricing"
- "No hidden fees"
- "You own your listing"
- "First booking within 1 week"

## Words/Phrases to Avoid
- "Cheap" (use "affordable" or "competitive")
- Overpromising specific dollar amounts
- Negative talk about competitors
- Overly corporate/formal language

## Competitor Comparison (use sparingly, factually)
| Us | Competitors |
|----|-------------|
| 12-15% fees | 18-25% fees |
| <9 min response | Hours or days |
| No long contracts | Lock-in periods |
| You own listing | Listing hostage |
| Direct contact | Group chats |
| Local experts | Not local |
| Transparent | Hidden fees |

## Call to Action
Primary CTA: "Get Your Free Estimate" or "Free Rental Estimate"
Secondary: "Book Your Consultation"
