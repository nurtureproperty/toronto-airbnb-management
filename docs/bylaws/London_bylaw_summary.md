# London Short-Term Rental By-law Summary

**By-law Number:** L.-131-16 (Business Licensing By-law), Schedule 21 - Short-Term Accommodations
**Effective Date:** October 2022
**Municipality:** City of London, Ontario

---

## Key Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Short-Term Accommodation (STA)** | Rental of sleeping accommodations for 29 consecutive days or less |
| **Provider** | Person who operates/provides an STA at their resident address |
| **Broker** | Person who advertises or facilitates STA reservations via internet/otherwise |
| **Resident Address** | Address where operator resides 8+ months per year with physical presence |
| **Reservation** | Booking commitment between provider and guest for specified time period |
| **Bedroom** | Room designed primarily for sleeping that meets Building Code requirements |

---

## Principal Residence Requirement

- **Required:** Yes - must be your "Resident Address"
- **Definition:** Property where you reside at least 8 months per calendar year with physical presence
- **Limit:** Maximum 2 STAs per provider (must be on same property as resident address)
- **Not Permitted:** Investment properties, second homes, or properties where owner doesn't reside

---

## Licensing Requirements

### Provider License

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **License Fee** | **$196/year** (not pro-rated) |
| **Validity** | 1 year |
| **Application Fee** | Included in license fee |

**Application Requirements:**
- Completed application in form approved by License Manager
- Business name (if applicable)
- Provider name, mailing address, email, phone
- Municipal address of STA
- Number of bedrooms to be rented (max 5)
- Declaration that property is Resident Address
- Proof of compliance with Fire Code
- Insurance certificate ($2M liability)
- Floor plan showing bedroom locations and square footage
- Payment of applicable fees
- Criminal Record and Judicial Matters Check (new applicants)

### Broker License

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **License Fee** | As prescribed in Schedule 1 |
| **Validity** | 1 year |
| **Insurance** | $5,000,000 Commercial General Liability |

**Application Requirements:**
- Completed application form
- If corporation: incorporating documents and annual return
- If partnership: partnership documents with names/addresses
- Business address in Ontario (not P.O. box)
- Designated representative name, phone, and email
- Proof of insurance
- Criminal Record and Judicial Matters Check (for individuals)

---

## Insurance Requirements

| Category | Coverage Required |
|----------|-------------------|
| **Providers** | $2,000,000 per occurrence liability |
| **Brokers** | $5,000,000 Commercial General Liability |

**Policy Requirements:**
- Include 30 days prior notice to City of cancellation
- Cover short-term rental activities
- Satisfactory to License Manager

**Alternative:** If using platforms like Airbnb with their own Host Guarantee, insurance certificate may not be required (at License Manager's discretion).

---

## Occupancy Limits

| Limit Type | Maximum |
|------------|---------|
| **STAs per Provider** | 2 (on same property) |
| **Bedrooms per STA** | 5 |
| **Bedroom Floor Area** | Min. 7 sq. meters primary + 4 sq. meters each additional person |

**Note:** Bedrooms must meet Building Code requirements for habitable rooms.

---

## Operating Requirements

### Advertising
- Must display license number prominently in all advertisements
- Must display maximum overnight guest limit
- Remove prohibited advertisements within 7 days of notice

### Guest Information Package
Must provide to guest at booking AND in printed form in unit:
- Contact info for provider/designate available 24/7
- Instructions for 9-1-1 emergency system
- Two nearest hospitals/emergency services
- Municipal services access information
- Non-emergency police contact
- Floor plan with evacuation routes
- Safety equipment locations
- Notice of any recording devices
- Waste disposal/recycling instructions
- Parking instructions
- Copy of license with occupancy limits

### Fire Safety
- Smoke alarms in accordance with Fire Code
- Carbon monoxide detectors (if fuel-burning appliances)
- Fire extinguisher on each floor
- Posted fire escape plan

### Record Keeping
Must maintain for 3 years:
- Number of nights rented
- Nightly and total price for each rental
- Guest information
- Complaint records

---

## Prohibited Activities

Providers shall NOT:
- Operate without a license
- Operate at non-resident address
- Exceed bedroom limits (5 max)
- Exceed guest limits
- Fail to display license number in ads
- Use unlicensed brokers
- Provide false information
- Obstruct inspections

---

## Broker Requirements

Licensed brokers must:
- Register with the City
- Maintain $5M liability insurance
- Have complaint handling procedures
- Remove unlicensed listings within 7 days of notice
- Provide data to City quarterly:
  - Municipal addresses of listings
  - Nights rented per listing
  - Revenue collected per listing
  - Complaints received
- Make records available within 7 days upon request
- Convey municipal communications to providers

---

## Enforcement

### Inspections
License Manager or designate may:
- Enter property at reasonable times
- Require production of documents
- Make examinations and take photos
- Audit records

### License Suspension/Revocation
License Manager may refuse, suspend, or revoke license for:
- False/misleading information
- Non-compliance with by-law
- Failure to meet application requirements
- Outstanding fines or fees
- Conduct adverse to public interest
- Health/safety concerns

---

## Penalties

| Violation Type | Fine Range |
|----------------|------------|
| **First Offence** | Starting at $500 |
| **Subsequent Offences** | Double previous fine |
| **Maximum per Offence** | Up to $100,000 |
| **Continuing Offences** | Per day penalties apply |

---

## Appeals Process

**Appeal Body:** License Appeal Committee

**Timeline:**
- Request review within 14 days of decision
- Hearing held at least 14 days after request received

**Process:**
- Applicant may be represented by counsel
- Can present evidence and cross-examine
- Committee decision is final and binding

---

## Municipal Accommodation Tax (MAT)

- **Rate:** 4%
- **Collection:** Required on all STA bookings
- **Remittance:** As prescribed by City
- **HST:** Applies on top of MAT

---

## Landlord/Condo Restrictions

- **Rental Properties:** Landlord consent required for tenants
- **Condominiums:** Must comply with condo corporation rules
- **Prohibitions:** Building owners may register prohibition with City

---

## Quick Reference for Hosts

1. Must be your resident address (live there 8+ months/year)
2. Obtain provider license ($196/year)
3. Maximum 2 STAs, 5 bedrooms each
4. Maintain $2M liability insurance
5. Display license number on all listings
6. Provide guest information package
7. Maintain records for 3 years
8. Use only licensed brokers/platforms
9. Follow Fire Code requirements
10. Renew annually

|---------|--------|---------|--------|
