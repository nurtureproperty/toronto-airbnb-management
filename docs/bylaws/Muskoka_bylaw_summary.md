# Muskoka Lakes Short-Term Rental Accommodation By-law Summary

**By-law Number:** 2025-049
**Title:** Short Term Rental Accommodation Licensing By-law
**Effective Date:** April 16, 2025 (Section 5.6 effective May 1, 2026)
**Municipality:** Township of Muskoka Lakes, Ontario

---

## Key Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Short Term Rental Accommodation (STRA)** | Use of premises for temporary accommodation for 28 consecutive days or less via concession, permit, lease, license, or rental agreement |
| **Premises** | The lot upon which STRA is operated including buildings/structures |
| **Responsible Person** | Person assigned by owner to ensure premises operates in compliance with by-law |
| **Primary Renter** | Person who entered into the rental agreement |
| **Summer Monthly Break** | 7 consecutive nights off required in each of June, July, and August |
| **Summer Rental Frequency** | Maximum one Primary Renter per 6 consecutive nights during summer season |

---

## UNIQUE MUSKOKA RULES: Summer Restrictions

### Summer Monthly Break (Effective May 1, 2026)
**Applies to:** Community Residential (R4), Waterfront Residential, and Waterbody Open Space zones

- **Requirement:** CEASE all rentals for 7 consecutive nights in EACH of:
  - June
  - July
  - August
- **Purpose:** Reduce rental intensity in cottage country during peak season

### Summer Rental Frequency (Effective May 1, 2026)
**Period:** Friday before Victoria Day through Tuesday after Labour Day

- **Requirement:** Maximum ONE Primary Renter per 6 consecutive nights
- **Effect:** Prevents rapid turnover and back-to-back short stays
- **Example:** If guests check out Saturday, next guests cannot check in until Thursday

**Important:** See Schedule 'C' in by-law for calendar examples of these rules.

---

## Principal Residence Requirement

- **Not Required:** Muskoka does NOT require STRAs to be principal residence
- **Owner-Occupied Not Required:** Investment properties/cottages eligible
- **Limit:** One STRA license per premises

---

## Licensing Requirements

### License Fees

| Category | Fee |
|----------|-----|
| **License Fee** | $1,000/year |
| **Early Application Discount** | 20% off if applied before September 30, 2025 |
| **Inspection Fees** | Additional (as per Fees and Charges By-law) |

### License Term
- Valid for 1 year from issuance
- Expires upon sale of property to new owner
- Annual renewal required

### Application Requirements

1. **Owner/Applicant Information:**
   - Names, addresses, phone numbers, emails
   - Written authorization if applicant acting for owner
   - Proof applicant is 18+ years
   - Corporate documents (if applicable)

2. **Property Documentation:**
   - Proof of ownership (deed, parcel register, tax bill)
   - If tenant: written owner consent
   - Photograph of front of STRA
   - Photograph of driveway entrance
   - Photograph of private road entrance (if applicable)

3. **Access Type Declaration:**
   - Year-round maintained public road
   - Seasonally maintained public road
   - Private road or Right of Way
   - Water access only

4. **Plans Required:**
   - **Site Map:** Scale drawing showing buildings, septic, parking, waste storage
   - **Floor Plan:** Dimensioned drawing showing:
     - Each room and intended use
     - Smoke and CO alarm locations with dates
     - Fire extinguisher locations
     - All entrances and exits
     - Fire escape routes
     - Fireplace locations
     - Cooking appliance locations

5. **Insurance:**
   - **$2,000,000 minimum liability**
   - Must identify STRA operation on policy
   - Certificate required

6. **Sworn Declaration:**
   - No outstanding Building Code orders
   - Functioning smoke alarms, CO alarms, fire extinguishers
   - Renters will receive information package and Code of Conduct
   - Insurance will be maintained throughout license period
   - Information is accurate and complete

## Occupancy Limits

| Rule | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| **Base Occupancy** | 2 persons per bedroom |
| **Increase Allowed** | If septic system designed for higher occupancy |
| **Children Exemption** | Children aged 2 and under not counted |
| **Proof for Increase** | Must demonstrate septic compliance with Ontario Building Code |

## Operating Requirements

### Responsible Person
- Must be designated by owner
- Must be available to respond to emergencies/complaints
- **Must arrive at STRA within 1 hour** of Township communication
- Contact info must be provided to Primary Renter

### Renter's Code of Conduct
Must be signed by Primary Renter, covering:
- Acknowledgment STRA is in residential area
- Treat premises as own home
- Respect neighbours
- Noise by-law compliance
- Fireworks restrictions (specific permitted days only)
- Access and parking requirements
- Recycling and garbage procedures
- Septic system limitations
- Open air fire rules (burning permit required April-October)
- Fire and occupant safety
- Boating and water safety

### Renter's Information Package
Must provide to Primary Renter (hard copy or electronic):
1. Municipal address
2. Responsible Person contact info
3. Emergency services contacts (911, OPP, nearest hospital)
4. Waste disposal info and addresses
5. Site Map
6. Floor Plan
7. Renter's Code of Conduct
8. Posted License
9. Township by-law offences and penalties schedule
10. Boater Safety Information
11. Any owner-specific rules

### Record Keeping
Maintain records available for inspection:
- Date of entry
- Length of stay
- Confirmation of Code of Conduct receipt
- Keep for license period + 2 months

### Advertising
- Display license number on ALL online advertisements
- Only advertise in compliance with license terms

### Property Maintenance
- Clean and sanitary condition
- Adequate waste storage (wildlife-proof container)
- Sufficient size to store all waste until collection
- Located outside STRA building

---

## Prohibited STRA Locations

STRAs are NOT permitted in:
- Buildings without habitable room (as defined in Zoning By-law)
- Motor vehicles, trailers, travel trailers, or tents
- Community housing
- Vessels or floating accommodation (docked or not)
- Buildings where STRA prohibited by Zoning By-law

## Demerit Point System

Muskoka uses a unique demerit point system for enforcement.

### Point Thresholds

| Total Points | Consequence |
|--------------|-------------|
| **10 points** | Must submit mitigation plan |
| **15 points** | License suspension notice issued |

### Points Duration
- Points remain for 3 years from date imposed

### Sample Infractions and Points

| Infraction | Points |
|------------|--------|
| Fire Code confirmed order | 15 |
| Building Code violation | 15 |
| Own/Market/Operate without license | 10 |
| Operate while suspended/revoked | 15 |
| Exceed maximum occupancy | 10 |
| Obtain license with false info | 10 |
| Fail to comply with Order | 10 |
| Hinder/obstruct Officer | 10 |
| Operate during Summer Monthly Break | 5 |
| Exceed Summer Rental Frequency | 5 |
| Fail to display license in ads | 5 |
| Fail to provide info package | 5 |
| Fail to maintain clean condition | 5 |
| Fail to keep rental records | 5 |

## Enforcement

### Inspections
Officers may enter at reasonable times to verify:
- By-law compliance
- License conditions
- Floor Plan and Site Map compliance
- Order compliance

**Inspection Powers:**
- Require document production
- Remove documents for copying
- Require information from any person
- Make examinations, tests, samples, photographs

### Orders
Officers may issue orders requiring:
- Compliance within specified time
- Work to be done (at licensee's expense if defaulted)
- Activity to be discontinued

## Penalties

| Offender | Maximum Fine |
|----------|--------------|
| **Individual** | $100,000 per offence |
| **Corporation/Director** | $100,000 per offence |
| **Continuing Offence** | $10,000 per day |
| **Multiple Offence** | $10,000 per offence |
| **Special Fine (economic gain)** | May exceed $100,000 |
| **Total Fines** | NOT limited to $100,000 |

## Appeals Process

**Appeal Body:** Township Appeals Committee (established under By-law 2025-007)

**Timeline:**
- Submit appeal within 30 calendar days of Order/decision
- Pay prescribed appeal fee

**Appealable Matters:**
- License suspension, refusal, or revocation
- Orders issued under by-law

**NOT Appealable:**
- Federal/Provincial law violations
- Other Municipal By-law violations
- Medical Officer of Health orders
- Immediate danger suspensions (Section 8.2)

## Quick Reference for Muskoka STRA Hosts

2. **NO principal residence requirement**
3. **$2M liability insurance required**
4. **Responsible Person required** (must arrive within 1 hour)
5. **Occupancy:** 2 persons per bedroom (unless septic permits more)
6. **Summer rules (from May 2026):**
   - 7 consecutive nights OFF in June, July, AND August
   - Only 1 Primary Renter per 6 nights during summer
7. **Display license number** on all ads
8. **Provide Renter's Information Package**
9. **Get signed Code of Conduct** from Primary Renter
10. **Keep records** (date, length of stay, Code receipt)
11. **Wildlife-proof waste storage required**
12. **Floor plan and site map required**
