# Niagara Falls Short-Term Rental By-law Summary

**By-law Number:** 2021-57 (as amended by 2025-033)
**Title:** Vacation Rental Units, Bed and Breakfasts, and Owner Occupied Short Term Rentals
**Effective Date:** Originally 2021, latest amendments 2025
**Municipality:** City of Niagara Falls, Ontario

---

## Key Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Vacation Rental Unit (VRU)** | Premises rented for 28 consecutive days or less, NOT principal residence |
| **Bed and Breakfast** | Owner-occupied dwelling with max 3 guest rooms, breakfast provided |
| **Owner Occupied Short Term Rental** | STR in owner's principal residence for 28 days or less |
| **Owner** | Registered owner or purchaser under Agreement of Purchase and Sale |
| **Principal Residence** | Single dwelling unit where owner resides minimum 6 months/year |
| **Short Term Rental Platform** | Online/electronic service that facilitates STR bookings |

---

## License Categories

### 1. Vacation Rental Unit (VRU)
- **NOT** principal residence requirement
- **Zoning Restricted:** Only in Tourist Commercial or General Commercial zones
- Entire property rented to guests
- Maximum 3 bedrooms

### 2. Bed and Breakfast (B&B)
- **Must be** owner's principal residence
- Owner lives on-site during guest stays
- Maximum 3 guest rooms
- Breakfast service provided

### 3. Owner Occupied Short Term Rental (NEW - 2025)
- **Must be** owner's principal residence
- Owner may or may not be present during rentals
- Maximum 3 bedrooms for STR use
- **150-meter separation distance** from other Owner Occupied STRs (in same zone)

---

## Principal Residence Requirements

| License Type | Principal Residence Required | Owner On-Site |
|--------------|------------------------------|---------------|
| VRU | NO | No |
| B&B | YES | YES (during stays) |
| Owner Occupied STR | YES (6 months/year minimum) | Optional |

## Licensing Requirements

### License Fees

| License Type | Initial Fee | Annual Renewal |
|--------------|-------------|----------------|
| **VRU** | $500 | $250 |
| **B&B** | $500 | $250 |
| **Owner Occupied STR** | $500 | $250 |

### Application Requirements (All Categories)

1. **Completed application form** with:
   - Owner name, address, phone, email
   - Property address
   - Number of bedrooms
   - Parking spaces available
   - Emergency contact (24/7 availability)

2. **Supporting Documents:**
   - Site plan showing:
     - Building location on lot
     - Parking spaces
     - Accessory structures
   - Floor plan showing:
     - Room dimensions
     - Bedroom locations
     - Safety equipment locations
   - Fire Chief Compliance Certificate
   - Electrical Safety Authority (ESA) Certificate
   - Proof of insurance ($2M liability)
   - Copy of Renter's Code of Conduct
   - Standard Rental Agreement

3. **Principal Residence Proof** (for B&B and Owner Occupied STR):
   - Property tax bill
   - Utility bills
   - Driver's license
   - Voter registration

### Fire Chief Compliance Certificate

Must demonstrate compliance with:
- Ontario Fire Code
- Smoke alarm requirements
- Carbon monoxide detector requirements
- Fire extinguisher placement
- Emergency evacuation routes posted

### ESA Certificate

Required electrical inspection confirming:
- Electrical systems meet Ontario Electrical Safety Code
- No hazards present
- Proper grounding and circuit protection

## Insurance Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Coverage Amount** | $2,000,000 minimum liability |
| **Type** | Comprehensive General Liability |
| **Named Insured** | Must include City of Niagara Falls |
| **Notice** | 30 days written notice of cancellation to City |

## Occupancy & Operating Limits

| Limit | Requirement |
|-------|-------------|
| **Maximum Bedrooms** | 3 per property |
| **Maximum Stay** | 28 consecutive days |
| **Parking** | Minimum 2 on-site spaces |
| **Guest Occupancy** | Per bedroom as approved on license |

---

## Zoning Restrictions

### VRU Permitted Zones:
- Tourist Commercial (TC)
- General Commercial (GC)
- NOT permitted in residential zones

### B&B Permitted Zones:
- As per Zoning By-law provisions
- Residential zones where permitted

### Owner Occupied STR:
- Residential zones where permitted
- **150-meter separation** from other Owner Occupied STRs in same zone

---

## Operating Requirements

### Renter's Code of Conduct

Must be provided to guests and include:
- Noise restrictions and quiet hours
- Parking requirements
- Garbage/waste disposal rules
- Maximum occupancy
- Prohibition on parties/events
- Emergency contact information
- Local by-law requirements

### Standard Rental Agreement

Must include:
- Guest acknowledgment of Renter's Code
- Noise by-law notification
- Occupancy limits
- Check-in/check-out times
- Emergency procedures

### Record Keeping

Maintain for minimum 3 years:
- Booking records
- Guest information
- Rental agreements
- Complaint records
- Financial records

### Advertising Requirements

- Display valid license number on ALL advertisements
- Include on all platforms (Airbnb, VRBO, etc.)
- Accurate bedroom count
- Accurate occupancy limits

---

## Municipal Accommodation Tax (MAT)

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Rate** | $2 flat fee per night |
| **Collection** | By operator or platform |
| **Remittance** | As prescribed by City |
| **Records** | Must maintain for audit |

---

## Enforcement

### Inspections
By-law Officers may:
- Conduct property inspections
- Require production of records
- Verify compliance with license conditions
- Issue orders for non-compliance

### License Conditions
Director may impose conditions on any license for:
- Public safety concerns
- Nuisance complaints
- Non-compliance history
- Neighbourhood impacts

### Suspension/Revocation

Grounds for license action:
- False information on application
- Failure to maintain insurance
- By-law violations
- Fire Code violations
- Nuisance complaints
- Failure to pay fees/fines

---

## Penalties

| Violation | Maximum Fine |
|-----------|--------------|
| **Per Offence** | Up to $100,000 |
| **Continuing Offence** | Additional daily fines |
| **Corporation** | Higher maximum fines |

---

## Appeals Process

**Appeal Body:** License Appeal Committee / Council

**Timeline:**
- Written appeal within 14 days of decision
- Hearing scheduled within reasonable time

**Applicant Rights:**
- Present evidence
- Be represented by counsel
- Cross-examine witnesses

## Key 2025 Amendments (By-law 2025-033)

1. **New Category:** Owner Occupied Short Term Rental added
2. **Separation Distance:** 150-meter requirement for Owner Occupied STRs
3. **Updated Definitions:** Clarified principal residence requirements
4. **Enhanced Enforcement:** Stricter compliance monitoring

## Quick Reference for Hosts

### VRU (Investment Property):
1. Property must be in Tourist/Commercial zone
2. License fee: $500 initial, $250 renewal
3. Fire Chief and ESA certificates required
4. $2M insurance required
5. Maximum 3 bedrooms
6. Collect $2/night MAT

### B&B (Live-In Owner):
1. Must be your principal residence
2. Must be on-site during guest stays
3. Provide breakfast
4. Maximum 3 guest rooms
5. Same licensing and safety requirements

### Owner Occupied STR (Principal Residence):
1. Live there 6+ months/year
2. Can rent while you're away
3. 150m separation from other Owner Occupied STRs
4. Maximum 3 bedrooms
5. Same licensing and safety requirements
