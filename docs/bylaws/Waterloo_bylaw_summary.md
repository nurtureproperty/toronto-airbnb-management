# Waterloo Rental Housing Licensing By-law Summary

**By-law Number:** 2011-047
**Title:** Rental Housing Licensing Bylaw
**Last Amended:** By-law 2023-056, June 12, 2023
**Effective Date:** April 1, 2012
**Municipality:** City of Waterloo, Ontario

---

## Key Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Short-Term Rental** | All or part of a dwelling unit for transient sleeping accommodations for 30 consecutive days or less, typically via platform; must be owner-occupied |
| **Short-Term Rental Platform** | Online service, website, or app that facilitates STR rental, advertisement, or listing |
| **Owner Occupied** | Occupied by persons holding at least 50% of the title as identified in Ministry Parcel Register |
| **Rental Unit** | A dwelling unit offered for rent |
| **Dwelling Unit** | Self-contained set of rooms in a building for residential use with kitchen and bathroom |
| **Local Contact** | Agent/representative for owners not residing in Region of Waterloo |

---

## Principal Residence Requirement

- **Required:** Yes - Short-Term Rentals must be "Owner Occupied"
- **Definition:** Property where owner(s) hold at least 50% of title and reside
- **License Class:** Class "B" License (owner-occupied properties)
- **Not Permitted:** Non-owner-occupied investment properties for STRs under 30 days

---

## License Classes Overview

| Class | Description | Max Bedrooms |
|-------|-------------|--------------|
| **Class A** | Non-owner-occupied rental units | 4 bedrooms |
| **Class B** | Owner-occupied rentals & Short-Term Rentals | 4 bedrooms |
| **Class C** | 5+ bedroom rental units (lodging house rules apply) | 5+ bedrooms |
| **Class D** | Grandfathered lodging houses | As per previous license |
| **Class E** | Temporary rental units (max 36 months) | Per application |
| **Class Z** | Buildings with 4+ horizontally separated units | Director discretion |

---

## Short-Term Rental Specific Requirements (Class B)

### Eligibility
- Must be Owner Occupied
- Maximum 30 consecutive days per rental
- **Maximum 275 days per calendar year** for STR operation
- Maximum 4 bedrooms for rent (unless grandfathered)
- No more than 50% of gross floor area can be bedrooms

### Operational Limits
| Limit | Requirement |
|-------|-------------|
| **Rental Period** | Maximum 30 consecutive days |
| **Annual Cap** | 275 days per calendar year |
| **Bedrooms** | Maximum 4 for rent |
| **Bedroom Floor Area** | Maximum 50% of dwelling unit's gross floor area |

## Licensing Requirements

### Application Requirements (All Classes)

1. **Property Information:**
   - Municipal address and legal description
   - Proof of ownership (transfer/deed)
   - Floor plan with room dimensions and uses

2. **Owner/Applicant Information:**
   - Name and contact information
   - If partnership: names of all partners
   - If corporation: names of directors, officers, 30%+ shareholders
   - Local Contact (if owner outside Region of Waterloo)

3. **Documentation Required:**
   - Police records check for owners/applicants
   - Parking plan compliant with Zoning By-law
   - Property maintenance plan
   - Signed compliance statement

4. **Insurance:**
   - **$2,000,000 minimum liability** per occurrence
   - Must identify STR business on policy
   - Certificate required with application

5. **Inspection Certificates:**
   - HVAC inspection certificate (certified technician)
   - Electrical Safety Authority (ESA) certificate (within 6 months)

### License Fees
- As per City's Fees and Charges By-law
- Application discount: 20% if applied before September 30, 2025

### License Term
- Expires March 31st following issuance
- Annual renewal required

## Property Standards

### Bedroom Floor Area Requirements

| Occupancy | Minimum Floor Area |
|-----------|-------------------|
| 3 adults | 14 square metres |
| 4+ adults | 7 square metres per adult |

### Other Requirements
- Post license copy in conspicuous place
- Post floor plan
- Comply with all applicable zoning
- Maintain property per Property Standards By-law
- Proper garbage/recycling storage

---

## Exemptions (License Not Required)

- Student residences operated by University of Waterloo or Wilfrid Laurier (BI zone)
- Apartment buildings (4+ horizontally separated units)
- Group homes
- Hotels, inns, bed and breakfasts
- Special care homes (provincial legislation)
- Long-term care homes
- Retirement homes
- Social housing (with Regional agreement)

---

## Enforcement

### Inspections
City may enter at reasonable times to verify:
- By-law compliance
- License conditions
- Orders under Municipal Act

**Inspection Powers:**
- Require document production
- Remove documents for copying
- Require information from any person
- Make examinations, take tests/samples/photos

### Grounds for License Refusal/Revocation

- Past conduct suggesting non-compliance
- Adverse to public interest
- Outstanding orders (Property Standards, Building Code, Fire Code, Health)
- Zoning non-compliance
- Outstanding fines/penalties/taxes to City
- Criminal conviction within past 5 years (not pardoned)
- Statutory/regulatory offence related to rental properties

---

## Penalties

| Offender | First Offence | Subsequent Offence |
|----------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Individual** | Min $350, Max $25,000 | Max $50,000 |
| **Corporation** | Max $50,000 | Max $100,000 |
| **Daily/Continuing** | $350 - $10,000 per day | No $100,000 cap on total |

**Additional Penalties:**
- Court may order premises closed up to 2 years
- Administrative penalties under Administrative Penalty By-law

---

## Appeals Process

**Appeal Body:** Licensing Appeal Tribunal

**Timeline:**
- Submit within 21 days of Director's decision
- Written submission with detailed grounds
- Pay applicable fee

**Tribunal Composition:**
- Minimum 3 members appointed by Council
- 3-year terms
- Council delegates final decision authority

**Tribunal Powers:**
- Confirm, modify, or rescind Director's decision
- Decision is final

---

## Quick Reference for STR Hosts

1. **Must be owner-occupied** (50%+ title ownership)
2. **Class B License required**
3. **Maximum 30 consecutive days** per rental period
4. **Maximum 275 days per year** total STR operation
5. **Maximum 4 bedrooms** for rent
6. **$2M insurance** required
7. **ESA electrical inspection** required
8. **Post license and floor plan** in visible location
9. **Local contact required** if owner outside Region
10. **Annual renewal** by March 31
