# Niagara-on-the-Lake Short-Term Rental By-law Summary

**By-law Number:** 2025-032
**Title:** Licensing, Regulating and Governing of Short Term Rentals
**Effective Date:** May 27, 2025
**Replaces:** By-law 4634-13 and amendments
**Municipality:** Town of Niagara-on-the-Lake, Ontario

---

## Key Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Bed and Breakfast** | Single-detached dwelling, max 3 guest bedrooms, owner lives on-site, breakfast only |
| **Cottage Rental** | Single-detached dwelling, max 3 guest bedrooms, NOT principal residence, up to 28 days |
| **Villa** | Single-detached dwelling, 4+ bedrooms, NOT principal residence, up to 28 days |
| **Country Inn** | Principal residence of owner, 3+ rooms, owner on-site, breakfast only, requires zoning amendment |
| **Vacation Apartment** | Apartment or residential unit above commercial business, up to 28 days |
| **Short Term Rental** | Any of the above - overnight lodging for up to 28 days |

---

## License Categories

| Type | Max Rooms | Principal Residence | Owner On-Site | Zoning Required |
|------|-----------|---------------------|---------------|-----------------|
| **Bed & Breakfast** | 3 | YES | YES | Standard |
| **Cottage Rental** | 3 | NO | NO | Standard |
| **Villa** | 4+ (max 6 in Agricultural) | NO | NO | Site-specific amendment |
| **Country Inn** | 3+ (max 6 in Agricultural) | YES | YES | Site-specific amendment |
| **Vacation Apartment** | Varies | NO | NO | Commercial zone |

---

## UNIQUE NOTL Rules: Dwelling Age Requirements

Properties must have been occupied as a single-detached dwelling for minimum years before applying:

| License Type | Minimum Dwelling Age |
|--------------|---------------------|
| **Cottage Rental** | 8 years |
| **Villa** | 8 years |
| **Country Inn** | 8 years |
| **Bed & Breakfast** | 4 years |
| **Vacation Apartment** | No requirement |

**Additions:** Any addition/extension expanding rooms cannot add new guest rooms until that portion has been occupied for minimum 4 years.

---

## Principal Residence Requirements

| License Type | Required? | Owner On-Site During Operation? |
|--------------|-----------|--------------------------------|
| **B&B** | YES | YES (full-time while guests present) |
| **Country Inn** | YES | YES (full-time while guests present) |
| **Cottage Rental** | NO | NO |
| **Villa** | NO | NO |
| **Vacation Apartment** | NO | NO |

**B&B/Country Inn Absence:** May appoint representative for maximum 30 days during emergency/vacation.

---

## License Fees and Term

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **License Period** | 4 years (expires Dec 31 of fourth year) |
| **Fee** | Per Town's Annual User Fees and Rates Schedule |
| **Prorated** | NO - full fee regardless of when applied |
| **Refundable** | NO (application fees non-refundable) |

### Refund Policy
- Cancel during 4-year term: may receive full refund for remaining years if:
  - Written cancellation received
  - Didn't operate in the year requesting refund
  - Request submitted by April 1st of refund year

## Application Requirements

### All License Types

1. **Completed application form**
2. **Site plan (to scale) showing:**
   - All buildings with setbacks from property lines
   - Parking area dimensions and spaces
   - Driveway access
   - Outdoor amenity area location/dimensions
3. **Insurance:** $2,000,000 liability minimum
   - Town of Niagara-on-the-Lake as "Additional Insured"
4. **Compliance with:**
   - Ontario Fire Code
   - Ontario Building Code
   - Property Standards By-law
   - Clean Yards By-law
   - Zoning By-laws
   - Ontario Electrical Safety Code

### Additional for B&B and Country Inn

- List of motor vehicles used/stored on-site with plate numbers
- Number of full-time occupants
- Proof of principal residence

### Country Inn and Villa Additional

- Approved planning applications (Site Specific Zoning Amendment, OPA, Site Plan Agreement) PRIOR to license application

## Insurance Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Minimum Coverage** | $2,000,000 liability |
| **Additional Insured** | "The Corporation of the Town of Niagara-on-the-Lake" |
| **Renewal** | Proof required for additional years of license |
| **Cancellation** | Must provide proof if policy cancelled |

## Occupancy Limits

**Maximum guests = (2 guests × number of bedrooms) + 2 additional guests**

Example: 3-bedroom property = (2 × 3) + 2 = 8 guests maximum

---

## Operating Requirements

### Required Documents for Guests (hard copy or electronic)
1. Copy of current license
2. Town's Noise By-law
3. Town's Public Nuisance By-law
4. Approved floor plans with exit routes
5. Renter's Code of Conduct

### Property Manager Requirement
If licensee not on-site while managing guests:
- Must provide property manager contact info
- Manager must be able to attend within **45 minutes** of contact

### Guest Registration
- Keep daily guest registration form
- Must include: name, room assigned, home address, phone, dates, vehicle info
- Available for inspection upon request

### B&B/Country Inn Specific
- Provide breakfast only
- No guest access to standard cooktops/ovens
- Owner/licensee must be on-site full-time while guests present

### Fire Safety
- Inspection by Fire Services minimum every 2 years
- Pay applicable user fees for inspections

### Prohibited Activities
- No events or commercial activities at STR premises

---

## Parking Requirements

- Based on Zoning By-law requirements PLUS:
- One additional space per vehicle registered to property
- On-street parking NOT permitted as compliance option
- Must show dimensions and setbacks on site plan
- Guests must park only in designated areas

---

## Advertising Requirements

- Must include valid license number on ALL advertisements
- Cannot advertise without license
- Cannot advertise without license number displayed

---

## Property Eligibility

Must meet before license will be issued:
- Municipal taxes current (not in arrears)
- Water and hydro accounts current
- Outstanding/open building permits closed and finalized
- Fully serviced with approved water and sewage
- Front onto Public Road or Niagara Parkway
- Only one STR per single-detached dwelling on residential property
- Accessory dwelling units NOT eligible
- If primary residence has accessory unit inside, cannot be licensed as cottage rental

---

## Multiple Units

- **Residential:** Only ONE STR per single-detached dwelling
- **Commercial:** Multiple Vacation Apartments may be permitted on one property

---

## Enforcement

### Grounds for Refusal/Revocation/Suspension

- Incorrect application information
- Non-compliance with Zoning By-law
- Outstanding fees
- Previous license revoked/suspended
- History of by-law contraventions
- Any breach of this by-law or other Town by-laws
- Refusal to comply with notice within 72 hours
- **3 or more complaints** about distinct events per calendar year

### After Revocation
No new license application considered for **minimum 12 months** from revocation date.

---

## Appeals Process

**Appeal Body:** Hearing Officer appointed by Council

**Timeline:**
- Submit Notice of Appeal within 14 days of refusal/revocation
- By registered mail to Administrator
- Pay non-refundable appeal fee

**Hearing Officer Powers:**
- Confirm or rescind Administrator's decision
- Order license issued/reinstated
- Confirm/modify/remove/add conditions
- Decision is final and binding

**NOT Appealable:**
- Incomplete applications
- Non-compliance with Zoning By-law

## Transition from Previous By-law

Licenses issued under By-law 4634-13 remain valid until their expiration date as indicated on the license.

---

## Legal Non-Conforming Status

When STR is sold:
- New owner has **1 year from possession date** to apply for license
- Maintains legal non-conforming status if applied within timeframe

## Quick Reference by License Type

### Bed & Breakfast
- Max 3 guest rooms
- Principal residence required
- Owner on-site required
- Dwelling age: 4 years minimum
- Breakfast only
- 4-year license

### Cottage Rental
- Max 3 guest rooms
- NOT principal residence
- Owner NOT required on-site
- Dwelling age: 8 years minimum
- Property manager within 45 min
- 4-year license

### Villa
- 4+ guest rooms (max 6 in Agricultural)
- NOT principal residence
- Owner NOT required on-site
- Dwelling age: 8 years minimum
- Requires site-specific zoning amendment
- 4-year license

### Country Inn
- 3+ rooms (max 6 in Agricultural)
- Principal residence required
- Owner on-site required
- Dwelling age: 8 years minimum
- Requires site-specific zoning amendment
- Breakfast only
- 4-year license

### Vacation Apartment
- Above commercial business
- NOT principal residence
- No dwelling age requirement
- Multiple permitted per commercial property
- 4-year license
